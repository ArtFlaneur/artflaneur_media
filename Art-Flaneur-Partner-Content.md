## 4. FAQ SECTION

### FOR GALLERIES

**FAQ Title:** "Everything You Need to Know"

**Q1: How long does it take to get my gallery on Art Flaneur?**

A: Free listing? Instant. We'll verify your details and have you live in 24-48 hours.

Premium tiers? Still fast. Set up your first analytics dashboard in about 30 minutes.

---

**Q2: Can I cancel anytime?**

A: Yes. No contracts, no penalties. Month-to-month billing, and you can downgrade or cancel from your dashboard.

---

**Q3: Do I need to submit my inventory or artworks?**

A: Nope. Art Flaneur is about visitor discovery and analytics‚Äînot inventory management.

Just tell us your opening hours, location, and exhibitions. We handle the rest.

---

**Q4: What data can I access in the analytics dashboard?**

A: With Insights Premium and above, you get:
- Real-time visitor count
- Where visitors came from (location data)
- How long they stayed in each room (heatmaps)
- Which exhibitions attracted the most traffic
- Visitor journey paths (which rooms they visited in order)
- Return visitor rate (how many came back)
- Export-ready reports for grants, boards, and funding applications

---

**Q5: Can I use the data for grants and sponsorship applications?**

A: Absolutely. Our reports are designed for exactly this. Show funders:
- Visitor numbers and demographics
- Exhibition impact (traffic increase with events)
- Audience trends over time
- Community engagement proof

Many of our partners use Art Flaneur analytics in Cultural Grants Australia applications.

---

**Q6: What if I have multiple gallery locations?**

A: Each location gets its own dashboard, but you can see combined analytics across all spaces with Premium or Studio tiers.

For 5+ locations, ask about our Studio/Enterprise package‚Äîwe'll set up a consolidated view.

---

**Q7: How is my visitor data collected? Is it private?**

A: Visitors opt-in when they use the Art Flaneur app. We don't track people outside the app.

Full GDPR and Australian Privacy Act compliance. You own your data.

---

**Q8: Do I pay for events separately, or are they included?**

A: Free Listing: No event listings.
Visibility Pro: Pay $89 per event listing.
Insights Premium: 1 free event listing per month, then $89 each.
Studio/Enterprise: Unlimited event listings included.

---

**Q9: Can I list past exhibitions or archive them?**

A: Yes. Keep past exhibitions on your profile as a portfolio, or hide them. You control visibility.

---

**Q10: What if my gallery is very small or emerging?**

A: Start free. No risk. Upgrade to Visibility Pro when you're ready ($249/month is designed for emerging spaces in regional Australia).

---

**Q11: How do I integrate with my website?**

A: We provide an embeddable widget and social media share links. Visitors can click straight through to Art Flaneur from your website.

For Studio/Enterprise: Full API access available.

---

**Q12: Do you offer discounts for non-profits or regional institutions?**

A: Yes. Ask us. We support emerging and non-profit galleries with flexible pricing.

Email: partnerships@artflaneur.com.au

### FOR EVENTS

**FAQ Title:** "Event Ticketing Questions Answered"

**Q1: How long does it take to list an event?**

A: 5 minutes. Fill in event details, upload a photo, set your ticketing option (RSVP or paid), and you're live.

---

**Q2: What payment methods do you accept?**

A: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Payment processing via Stripe. Funds transfer to your account within 2-3 business days.

---

**Q3: What's the difference between RSVP Listing and Event Trail?**

A: **RSVP Listing ($89):**
- Free event, no tickets
- Capacity tracking (see who's coming)
- Basic listing on app

**Event Trail ($299 + optional 2.5% transaction):**
- Full ticketing system
- Interactive map with multiple locations
- Real-time analytics & heatmaps
- Geo-fencing push notifications
- QR code check-in

---

**Q4: Why would I choose 2.5% transaction fee instead of flat $299?**

A: If you expect high ticket sales (200+ tickets), transaction-based is cheaper.

If you expect low attendance, flat fee is safer.

Example:
- 50 tickets @ $50 each = $2,500 revenue
  - Flat fee: $299 (6% cost)
  - Transaction (2.5%): $62.50 (2.5% cost)
  
- 150 tickets @ $50 each = $7,500 revenue
  - Flat fee: $299 (4% cost)
  - Transaction (2.5%): $187.50 (2.5% cost)

Talk to our team about which model suits your event size.

---

**Q5: Can I offer discount codes or early-bird pricing?**

A: Yes. With Event Trail and above, you can set:
- Early-bird discount (% off if purchased before date X)
- Promotional codes (fixed discount or % off)
- Group rates
- Different ticket types (student, general, VIP, etc.)

---

**Q6: What if I don't sell many tickets?**

A: RSVP Listing is for you. $89 one-time, no payment processing. Track attendance without fees.

---

**Q7: Can I refund tickets?**

A: Yes. You can issue refunds directly from the dashboard. We'll process them back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

---

**Q8: Does Art Flaneur show my event to people outside the app?**

A: Yes. Events also appear on:
- Art Flaneur website calendar (discoverable via search)
- Your shareable event link (post on socials, email, website)
- Art Flaneur push notifications to subscribers
- Art Flaneur weekly newsletter

---

**Q9: Can I see real-time ticket sales and attendance?**

A: Yes. With Event Trail and above, your dashboard updates in real-time.

See:
- Tickets sold (cumulative)
- Revenue generated
- Visitors checked in at venue (if using QR codes)
- Attendance vs. capacity

---

**Q10: What if my event is cancelled or rescheduled?**

A: No penalty. Update your event details anytime from the dashboard.

For refunds: You can issue full refunds to all ticket buyers with one click.

---

**Q11: Can I see which visitors came from Art Flaneur vs. other channels?**

A: With Event Trail analytics, yes.

We track:
- Total visitors to your event page in-app
- Ticket sales sourced from Art Flaneur
- Geographic origin of attendees

---

**Q12: Do you handle accessibility (dietary, mobility, etc.)?**

A: You can add custom questions at checkout:

- Accessibility needs
- Preferred seating
- etc.

Answers are sent to you immediately, and also saved in attendee profiles.

**Q13: What's included in the Festival package?**

A: Up to 40-50 event locations, professional video/photo curation, heatmaps, push notifications, newsletter features, and ambassador reviews.

Best for biennales, art weeks, and multi-venue festivals.

Ask for a custom demo if you're planning a large event.

**Q14: Can I customize the look and feel?**
